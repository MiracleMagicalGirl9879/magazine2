Pornography, Social Media and Teens

The rise of social media had caused many teenagers to use it often in their daily lives like interacting with family, friends, and entertainment but there are risks that is within the screen. This will show how unsupervised teens get exposed to inappropriate websites, content. Content could be sensitive and is advisable to be read with an open mind.

A big concern in social media is that anything on the internet can be shown to anyone and provided by anyone. No matter the age of the viewer or how they think. Social media is one of the factors that could affect the mental stability of teenagers and the way they think about others.

Most of the time teenagers are unsupervised by their parents on what they view which leads to expose them to various contents that could affect their mental health like gore, pornography and etc. Many social media apps like Tiktok, Facebook, Instagram and Snap Chat have a quite poor moderation when it comes to content since there are some content creators that promote pornography which is very horrible for the developmental stage of the human brain and how they think. A lot of exposure to these types of content could lead to porn addiction where the viewer cannot stop looking at these inappropriate videos or photos

Being exposed to these contents in such an early age could cause a big impact towards the viewers own self-concept in relationships, self-esteem and body image. This could also increase cases of grooming, sexual harassment, cyber bullying, pedophilia and inapproapriate views towards the opposite gender or same gender

All of this can affect the mental health and psychological health of teenagers which could lead to them making hasty decisions to fit into societal norms that they believe to try and fit in. This could also risk misinformation and misguide leading about their sexuality, and other genders. These types of content can cause the viewer to internalize what they see and perceive the fed information as true.

In conclusion this doesn't only rely on complete prevention but teenagers need to be educated about what they see. It is much better to give awareness than just preventing since there will always be a way for teenagers to get exposed towards these sensitive content. You can run away and hide from these contents but they will always find a way to capture you.
